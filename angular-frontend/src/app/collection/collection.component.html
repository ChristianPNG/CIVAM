White Pouch

The pouch is U-shaped with a long a bit narrowing neck. Made of tanned skin and lined with green cotton. There is a leather carrying strap. Beaded on both sides using lazy stitch and commercial yarn. The front has red and blue designs on a white background and accents with small round faceted brass beads. The back has green designs on a white background. The pouch is edged with powder blue beads and the neck with yellow. There is a stripe of blue and a few crystal beads across the neck. This item is attributed either to the Crow or Ute.
